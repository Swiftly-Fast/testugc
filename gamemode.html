Starting Positions

Runner team choose where they start in forest, tagger team starts on gazebo. When runner team is ready, the referee will say "runners run" giving the runners a 3 second head start. After the 3 seconds are up, the referee will say "taggers tag" releasing the taggers. The tagger teams task is to tag the runner team as quick as possible, the runner teams task is to run for as long as possible from the tagger team.

Match Duration

The team that gets the longest run time when both teams have ran and tagged, wins the round. All matches goes first to five points, if both teams agree the match can go first to three points. Timecap is always 3 minutes every round/match.
