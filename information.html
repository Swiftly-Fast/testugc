About Ultimate Gorilla Tag

UGT is a VR esports league, focusing on Gorilla Tag and its one-of-a-kind movement system. UGT unites players of all skills and is the hub for all competitive Gorilla Tag players.

Our league offers intense, fast-paced matches with a gamemode designed just for UGT. Whether you're a seasoned veteran or a newcomer, you'll find opportunities to attempt to compete in UGT.
